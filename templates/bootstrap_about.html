Tired of arguing with your partner over which movie you'll both want to watch? No more!

Date Night is a recommendation engine for two people.

As an item-based recommendation engine, Date Night asks just for your favorite movie and your partner's favorite movie. The site uses a predictive model to evaluate which other movies in its dataset have the highest similarity with both your selected movie and your partner's. To learn more, check out my slides.

I gratefully acknowledge the GroupLens team at the University of Minnesota for providing the data to make this project possible, and the fellows and staff of The Data Incubator for their support.
